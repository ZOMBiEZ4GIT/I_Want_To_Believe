# RECORDING NOTES: Episode 066 - Flatwoods Monster

## Pre-Recording Prep
- Key dramatic beats to emphasize:
  - Witness testimonies and firsthand accounts
  - Government involvement and potential cover-ups
  - Physical evidence and documentation discussion
  - The core incident/encounter narrative
  - Skeptical challenges and alternative explanations

- Voices needed:
  - Witness testimonies (vary pitch and emotion)
  - Skeptical arguments (rational, questioning)

- Complex sections to mark:
  - Scientific terminology and concepts
  - Location and proper name pronunciations
  - Historical dates and timeline sequences

- Rant opportunities:
  - Dismissive attitudes toward legitimate questions

## Timing Targets
- Cold open: 2-3 min
- Believer section: 24-26 min
- Skeptic section: 21-23 min
- Your take: 7-9 min

## Energy Map
- High energy:
  - Cold open - hook the audience immediately
  - Key incident description

- Conversational:
  - Background context and setup
  - Transition between sections

- Serious/dramatic:
  - Evidence analysis
  - Skeptical counterarguments

- Light/humorous:
  - Personal anecdotes or relatable moments

## Music Suggestions
- Intro: Mysterious, engaging sci-fi atmosphere
- Transition: Brief musical sting to signal perspective shift, then softer bed
- Background: Sparse tension during witness testimonies, silence for dramatic reveals

## Potential Challenges
- Scientific terminology and concepts
