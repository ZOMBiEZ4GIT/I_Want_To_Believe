## XP SYSTEM

### Task XP Values

**Learning & Prep:**
- Read episode brief: **10 XP**
- Read full framework: **25 XP**
- Mark up recording notes: **15 XP**
- Practice voices/pronunciations: **10 XP**
- Watch tutorial: **15 XP**
- Organize episode files: **5 XP**

**Recording:**
- Record cold open: **20 XP**
- Record believer section: **30 XP**
- Record skeptic section: **30 XP**
- Record your take: **20 XP**
- Record outro: **10 XP**
- **Complete full episode recording:** **100 XP** (replaces individual section XP)

**Editing:**
- Basic edit (cut mistakes/pauses): **30 XP**
- Audio processing (compression/EQ): **20 XP**
- Add music/effects: **10 XP**
- Final export: **15 XP**
- **Complete episode edit:** **50 XP** (if doing all at once)

**Publishing:**
- Upload to hosting: **25 XP**
- Write show notes: **10 XP**
- Create social posts: **10 XP**
- Submit to directories: **15 XP**
- **Complete episode publish:** **75 XP**

**Administrative:**
- Update completion tracker: **5 XP**
- Plan next episode: **5 XP**
- Batch prep 3 episodes: **25 XP**
- Organize workspace: **10 XP**

### Bonus XP

**Time-Based Bonuses:**
- Complete episode on schedule: **+25 XP**
- Record 2 episodes in one week: **+50 XP**
- Maintain 7-day work streak: **+100 XP**
- Maintain 30-day work streak: **+300 XP**

**Quality Bonuses:**
- Complete difficult episode (4-5 star difficulty): **+50 XP**
- Re-record to improve quality: **+25 XP**
- Implement new technique: **+20 XP**

**Comeback Bonuses:**
- Return after 2+ week break: **+50 XP**
- Complete episode after struggling: **+30 XP**

### Standard Episode XP

**One complete episode (start to finish):**
- Prep: 10 + 25 + 15 = 50 XP
- Recording: 100 XP
- Editing: 50 XP
- Publishing: 75 XP

**TOTAL: ~275 XP per episode**

With bonuses, you can earn 300-400 XP per episode.

## MOTIVATION STRATEGIES

### When Motivation Is High

**Ride the wave:**
- Record multiple sections
- Batch prep episodes
- Build buffer of completed episodes
- Bank XP for dry periods

### When Motivation Is Low

**Lower the bar:**
- Do just ONE 5-minute task
- Read one episode brief
- Update one tracker
- That's enough for today

**Review progress:**
- Look at completed episodes
- Count your XP
- See how far you've come
- Remember: you're further than most ever get

### When Motivation Is Zero

**It's okay to rest:**
- Take a week off
- No guilt
- Come back refreshed
- The episodes will wait

**But if you can:**
- Do literally any one task
- Mark one checkbox
- Something > nothing
- Break the zero streak
