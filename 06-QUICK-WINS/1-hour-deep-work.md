## HYPERFOCUS PROTECTION

**When you're in deep focus:**

**DO:**
- Ride the wave as long as it lasts
- Batch similar tasks together
- Produce as much as you can
- Bank work for low-energy days

**DON'T:**
- Burn out by pushing too hard
- Skip breaks if session goes long
- Sacrifice quality for speed
- Forget to track your progress

**REMEMBER:**
- Hyperfocus is a superpower
- But it's also exhausting
- Rest after big sessions
- Don't expect this energy daily

---

## THE 60-MINUTE SPRINT

**Structure for maximum productivity:**

**Minutes 0-5:** Setup
- Clear workspace
- Open all needed files
- Set environment

**Minutes 5-50:** Deep work
- No distractions
- Phone off
- Full focus
- Don't stop

**Minutes 50-55:** Wrap up
- Save everything
- Quick notes on what's done
- Note what's next

**Minutes 55-60:** Break
- Stand up
- Move
- Hydrate
- Celebrate

## MAXIMIZE GOOD DAYS

**When you have high focus:**

1. **Identify it early** - "Today is a good brain day"
2. **Clear your schedule** - Protect the time
3. **Pick high-value task** - Make it count
4. **Eliminate distractions** - Phone off, no social media
5. **Track your output** - See what you accomplished
6. **Rest afterward** - Honor the energy spent

**XP Potential in 1 hour:**
- Full episode recording: 100+ XP
- Full episode editing: 50+ XP
- Deep prep session: 50+ XP

**One focused hour = massive progress.**
