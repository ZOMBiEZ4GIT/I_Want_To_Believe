## CONFIDENCE BUILDING STRATEGY

### Episodes 1-3: Learn the Basics
- Pick from easiest 5
- Focus on completing, not perfecting
- Celebrate finishing

### Episodes 4-10: Build Skills
- Try different types (abduction, sighting, debunk)
- Work on pacing and voice
- Get comfortable with format

### Episodes 11-20: Find Your Style
- Mix easy and medium difficulty
- Develop your unique approach
- Build efficiency

### Episodes 21+: You're a Pro
- Tackle harder episodes
- Innovate and experiment
- Enjoy the process

---

## FINAL TIPS

**DO:**
- Start with episodes that excite you
- Pick cases you already know something about
- Choose topics that match your current energy
- Build from easy to hard

**DON'T:**
- Start with Roswell (too complex)
- Do episodes in numerical order (bad difficulty curve)
- Pick obscure cases first (harder to research)
- Choose super long episodes when starting

**REMEMBER:**
- There's no "correct" order
- This list is a guide, not rules
- Pick what excites you
- Have fun!
