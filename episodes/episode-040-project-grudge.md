### GRUDGE = APPROPRIATE RESPONSE TO HYSTERIA

**Skeptic Argument:**

**Context (1947-1949):**
- **UFO craze:** Thousands of reports (vast majority misidentifications)

"Grudge was PR management. Not cover-up."

### THE "ABSURD" EXPLANATIONS = ACTUALLY CORRECT (MOSTLY)

**Skeptic Defense:**

**Gorman Dogfight:**
- **Balloon explanation:** Partially correct (weather balloon tracked in area)
- **Pilot's maneuvers:** Created illusion of balloon "chasing" him (relative motion)
- **Not absurd:** Plausible

**Mantell Incident:**
- **Skyhook balloon:** CONFIRMED by later research (secret Navy balloon program, Mantell wouldn't have known)
- **Venus explanation:** Mistake (Grudge later corrected)
- **Not cover-up:** Error, then correction

**Lubbock Lights:**
- **Bird explanation:** Plausible (plovers flying over city, reflecting lights = common phenomenon)
- **Scientists rejected:** Doesn't mean impossible (scientists can be wrong)

**Skeptic Point:**
"Believer cherry-picks errors. Most Grudge explanations correct."

**Skeptic Response:**

**Ruppelt's Bias:**

**Grudge's Actual Record:**
