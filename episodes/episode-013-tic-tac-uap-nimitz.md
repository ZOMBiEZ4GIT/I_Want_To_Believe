**The Theory:**
- Radar glitch created false targets ("ghosts")
- Fravor saw weather balloon or distant aircraft
- Brain + expectation filled in details
- FLIR video shows mundane object (bird, balloon, distant jet)

**How Radar Glitches Work:**
- **Multipath propagation:** Radar bounces off water, creates false targets
- **Atmospheric ducting:** Temperature inversions bend radar, show objects at wrong locations
- SPY-1 is advanced, but not infallible

**Mick West's Analysis (Skeptic Investigator):**
- Video shows rotation consistent with gimbal lock (camera artifact)
- Object's movement could be parallax (camera moving, object stationary)
- **"Acceleration" at end:** Camera losing lock, not object speeding away

**Fravor's Visual Sighting:**
- Distance estimation at sea = notoriously difficult (no reference points)
- Could've seen weather balloon at distance (appears smooth, white)
- Movement could be aircraft at extreme distance (appears to "zip" due to perspective)

**Counterargument (Believers):**
- Multiple sensors confirmed same object (not just radar)
- Fravor got within ~1,000 feet (close enough to rule out balloon)
- Water disturbance suggests something real, not illusion

**Problems With This Theory:**
