**THE MOMENT:**
December 1984. A roll of undeveloped 35mm film arrives anonymously in the mail. No return address. Sent to Jaime Shandera, a TV producer and UFO researcher.

## ðŸ”¬ SKEPTIC'S RESPONSE (22 min)

### THE DOCUMENTS ARE FORGERIES (Here's How We Know)

**Red Flag #1: FORMATTING ERRORS**

**"TOP SECRET/MAJIC" Classification:**
- Real classification: "TOP SECRET" (various caveats)
- **"MAJIC" never used** (not in any verified government document)
- Likely typo: Should be "MAGIC" (real WWII codeword) or invented term

**Date Format:**
- Document uses "18 November, 1952"
- **Real military docs:** "18 November 1952" (no comma)
- **Consistent error** = forger didn't know military style

**Typewriter Font:**
- Forensic analysis: Typed on 1980s typewriter (NOT 1950s)
- Paper watermark: Not consistent with 1952 government paper

**Red Flag #2: SIGNATURE ANALYSIS**

**Truman's Signature:**
- "Truman memo" signature analyzed by experts
- **Doesn't match verified Truman signatures from 1947**
- Appears to be **traced from another document**
- Possible source: Truman letter to Vannevar Bush (1947) on unrelated matter

**Forrestal's Signature:**
- Spacing, slant don't match authenticated Forrestal signatures

**Red Flag #3: THE CUTLER-TWINING MEMO (Smoking Gun of Forgery)**

**What Happened:**
- 1985: Researcher finds "Cutler-Twining memo" in National Archives
- References MJ-12 meeting (July 1954)
- **Believers celebrated:** "See! It's in the Archives! Must be real!"

**Problem:**
- National Archives investigated
- **Conclusion:** Document is FAKE, planted in Archives
- **How they know:**
  - Wrong paper stock (not 1954 government paper)
  - Filed in wrong box (misfiled for document from that era)
  - Onion-skin paper (not used for memos in 1954)
- **Someone PLANTED it** to corroborate the MJ-12 story

**Red Flag #4: DR. MENZEL'S INCLUSION (Makes No Sense)**

**The Problem:**
- Donald Menzel = public UFO debunker (wrote books dismissing UFOs)

**Red Flag #5: ANACHRONISMS**
