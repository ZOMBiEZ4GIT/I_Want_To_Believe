## ðŸ”¬ SKEPTIC'S RESPONSE (22 min)

### THE TWO EVENTS EXPLAINED SEPARATELY

**Event #1 (8 PM V-Shape): Formation of Aircraft**

**The Evidence:**
- **Operation Snowbird:** Air National Guard confirmed planes flying in formation that night
- Maryland ANG A-10 Warthogs on training exercise
- Flight path: Davis-Monthan AFB (Tucson) to Nellis AFB (Nevada) and back
- Timing matches witness reports
- A-10s fly in V-formation

**Why Witnesses Misidentified:**
- **Dark sky + formation lights = illusion of solid object**
- Brain fills in "craft" between lights (pareidolia)
- Distance/altitude difficult to judge at night
- Witnesses assume lights connected = one object

**Mitch Stanley's Telescope Observation:**
- Amateur astronomer in Phoenix
- Viewed "craft" through 10" telescope
- **Saw:** Individual planes in formation, not solid craft
- **Quote:** "I saw planes. I'm certain of it."
- **Debunker argument:** Only person who used optical magnification saw planes

**Event #2 (10 PM Lights): Military Flares**

**Maryland ANG Official Explanation:**
- 10:00 PM: A-10s dropped LUU-2B/B illumination flares
- Location: Barry Goldwater Range (south of Phoenix)
- Purpose: Training exercise
- Flares drift on parachutes, burn for minutes
- Formation matches witness videos

**Cogniaux Report (Air National Guard Investigation):**
- Released months after incident
- Confirmed flare drop at 10 PM
- Flight records, pilot logs support this
- Timing, location match videos

**Why It Looks Strange:**
- Flares dropping over mountains create horizon-level illusion
- Parachute drift causes slight movement
- Burn time explains duration
- Distance (60+ miles) explains visibility from Phoenix and Tucson

### PROBLEMS WITH BELIEVER NARRATIVE

**Issue #1: Conflating Two Events**
- Witnesses often describe 8 PM craft but show 10 PM video
- Creates false impression videos show craft
- Most famous videos are clearly stationary lights (consistent with flares)

**Issue #2: Witness Reliability**
- **Size estimates wildly vary:** "football field" to "mile wide"
- **Altitude estimates vary:** 500 ft to 10,000 ft
- **No consensus on details** beyond "V-shape with lights"
- Human perception terrible at estimating size/distance/altitude at night

**Issue #3: No Radar Evidence**
- Luke AFB, Phoenix Sky Harbor Airport - no radar contacts
- If mile-wide craft at 500-1,000 ft, radar WOULD detect it
- Lack of radar = not a solid physical craft

**Issue #4: Governor Symington's Credibility**
- 2007 admission came during UFO documentary promotion
- Stands to benefit from UFO community support
- 1997 mockery may have been genuine skepticism
- **Skeptic view:** Changed story for attention/sympathy

**Issue #5: Mitch Stanley (Only Magnified View)**
- Only person to use telescope
- Saw individual aircraft
- Ignored by believers because inconvenient
- **Why trust hundreds of naked-eye witnesses over one with magnification?**

### PSYCHOLOGICAL FACTORS

**Mass Suggestion:**
- First witnesses call news stations
- Others hear reports, go outside, see formation of lights
- Expectation + poor depth perception = "craft"
- Social confirmation bias

**Pareidolia:**
- Brain connects unrelated lights into patterns
- V-formation of planes = brain fills in "solid object"
- Classic cognitive illusion

**Memory Reconstruction:**
- Event happened 1997
- Many accounts given years later (after UFO documentary exposure)
- Memory incorporates post-event narratives
- **Original reports** often less dramatic than later accounts
