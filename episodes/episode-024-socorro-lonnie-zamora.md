## ðŸ”¬ SKEPTIC'S RESPONSE (22 min)

### THE HOAX THEORIES

**Theory 1: STUDENT PRANK (Most Popular Skeptic Explanation)**

**The Claim:**
- New Mexico Tech students (Socorro) staged hoax
- Motive: Prank on Zamora (who'd ticketed students before)
- Method: Hot air balloon OR contraption with flares

**Supporting "Evidence":**
- **Lyle Boyd (NM Tech student):** Later claimed (1968) students discussed hoax
- Student **Stirling Colgate** (later astrophysicist) allegedly involved in planning

**How It Would Work:**
- Build balloon/contraption with flame effect (flares, propane)
- Launch in desert
- Zamora sees it, investigates
- **Students create fake landing site** (burn bushes, make depressions)

**Problems With This Theory:**

**1. Timing:**
- Zamora's backup arrived **minutes** later
- **No time** for students to create burn marks, depressions, escape unseen

**2. No Confession:**
- No student EVER officially confessed (only rumors)
- **Colgate denied involvement** (before his death)
- Lyle Boyd's claim secondhand (not witness)

**3. Physical Evidence:**
- **Burn pattern** inconsistent with campfire/flares
- Landing depressions: **Too precise** for quick fabrication
- FBI/Air Force found no tracks, no equipment

**4. Figures:**
- Zamora saw TWO small figures
- **Timing:** Figures disappeared when Zamora looked away (seconds)
- Hard to fake with students

**Skeptic Hedge:**
"Probably students, but no proof."

**Theory 2: EXPERIMENTAL AIRCRAFT**

**The Claim:**
- Secret military/government test
- **Lunar lander prototype?** (1964 = early space program)
- White Sands Proving Ground nearby (military test site)

**Supporting Logic:**
- Vertical takeoff/landing = experimental tech (VTOL research)
- Blue flames = rocket exhaust
- **Secrecy:** Government wouldn't confirm classified test

**Problems:**

**1. No Matching Projects:**
- **NASA:** No lunar lander tests in Socorro (tests at Edwards AFB, Cape Canaveral)
- **Military:** No VTOL tests match description

**2. Figures:**
- Experimental craft = pilots
- **Pilots would be adults** (not 4-5 foot figures)

**3. Air Force Investigation:**
- **Project Blue Book IS Air Force**
- If military test, they'd KNOW (and close case "identified")
- Classified as "unidentified" suggests NOT military

**Skeptic Hedge:**
"Maybe still-classified test. We'll never know."

**Theory 3: HELICOPTER MISIDENTIFICATION**

**The Claim:**
- Small helicopter (private or military)
- Zamora misperceived shape/details

**Problems:**

**1. Wrong Description:**
- Helicopters have rotors, tail (Zamora described smooth egg)
- Sound: Helicopter = distinctive rotor chop (Zamora: roar, then silence)

**2. Burn Marks:**
- Helicopters don't produce ground burns

**Skeptic Abandons This:**
"Doesn't fit. Not helicopter."
