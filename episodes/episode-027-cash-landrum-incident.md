**THE MOMENT:**
December 29, 1980, 9:00 PM. Betty Cash, Vickie Landrum, and Vickie's 7-year-old grandson Colby are driving home through rural Texas. Dark, isolated road through Piney Woods.

Ahead: **A massive diamond-shaped object descending from the sky. Flames shooting from the bottom. Bright, blinding. Hovering over the road.**

**Betty's conclusion:** "The government did this to us."

**She sues the U.S. Air Force, Army, NASA. Demands $20 million.**

**Was it a UFO? A secret military test? Or something else?**

**Most Common Skeptic Explanation:**

**What Actually Happened:**
- **Military helicopter exercise** (possibly classified)
- **Flare drop:** Magnesium flares (training or test)
- Witnesses saw: Bright flare descending, helicopters nearby

**How This Explains Sighting:**
- **"Diamond shape":** Flare parachute + bright light = distorted shape
- **"Flames":** Burning magnesium flare
- **Heat:** Radiant heat from flare (magnesium burns ~3,000Â°F)
- **Helicopters:** Part of exercise

**Why Government Denies:**
- **Classified exercise:** Can't acknowledge
- **Plausible deniability:** "No helicopters" (officially)

**Problems With This Theory:**

**1. Witness Description:**
- Flares descend quickly (witnesses saw hovering object for 15 min)
- **Shape:** Flares don't look diamond-shaped (parachute + light)

**2. Heat Duration:**
- Flares burn out in minutes
- **Witnesses:** Heat for extended period

**3. Government Denial Under Oath:**
- **Court case:** Military swore no operations
- **Perjury risk** (why lie under oath for flare drill?)

**Skeptic Hedge:**
"Maybe classified test. Can't prove because still secret."
