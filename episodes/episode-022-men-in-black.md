## ðŸ“– OVERVIEW

**The Phenomenon:**
- Men in Black (MIB): Mysterious individuals who visit UFO witnesses
- Typically: Black suits, outdated cars, odd behavior, threatening messages
- Appear after sightings (often before witness goes public)
- Goal: Intimidate witnesses into silence

**Believer position:** Government agents enforcing UFO cover-up OR non-human entities disguised as humans; too many consistent reports to dismiss; real intimidation tactics

**Skeptic position:** Folklore/urban legend; hoaxes; misidentified government investigators (real Men in Black = Air Force OSI); cultural contamination creates consistent descriptions

**Cultural impact:** Defined "government conspiracy" aesthetic; spawned movies, comics, memes
