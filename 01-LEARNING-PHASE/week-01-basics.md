# WEEK 1: BASICS & SETUP
## Getting Started - Don't Overthink It

**Goal:** Set up equipment, learn basics, record first test.
**Time:** 7-10 hours total across the week
**Outcome:** Ready to record your first episode next week

---

## MONDAY-TUESDAY: Equipment (2-3 hours)

### What You Actually Need (Minimum):
- **Microphone:** USB mic ($50-150) or what you have
- **Headphones:** Any closed-back headphones
- **Recording software:** Audacity (FREE) or GarageBand (Mac, free)
- **Computer:** What you already have
- **Quiet space:** Closet, bedroom, anywhere reasonably quiet

### DON'T:
- Research for more than 2 hours
- Buy expensive gear yet
- Wait for "perfect" setup

### DO:
- Order/acquire ONE decent USB microphone
- Download Audacity (audacityteam.org)
- Test what you have first

**Read:** `02-PRODUCTION-HUB/equipment-setup.md` (sections 1-3 only)

---

## WEDNESDAY-THURSDAY: Skills (2-3 hours)

### Watch Tutorials:
- "How to record a podcast in Audacity" (any 15-min video)
- "Basic podcast editing" (any 20-min video)
- "Mic setup and positioning" (any 10-min video)

### Practice:
- Set up your recording space
- Position microphone correctly (6-8 inches, slightly off-axis)
- Test levels (-12 to -6 dB)
- Record yourself talking for 5 minutes about anything
- Listen back (yes, you'll hate your voice - normal)

**Output:** One 5-minute test recording

---

## FRIDAY: First Edit (2-3 hours)

### Learn to Edit:
- Open your test recording in Audacity
- Cut out one long pause
- Remove one mistake
- Apply: Noise Reduction → Compressor → Normalize
- Export as MP3

### What You're Learning:
- Basic editing interface
- How to cut/trim
- Basic audio processing
- Export process

**Output:** One edited 5-minute MP3 file

---

## WEEKEND: Explore & Prep

### OPTIONAL Tasks:
- Browse episode list in `03-EPISODES/`
- Read a few EPISODE-BRIEF.md files
- Pick which episode you want to try first
- Get excited!

### Rest:
- You did great this week
- Take guilt-free rest
- Come back Monday ready to pick your first real episode
