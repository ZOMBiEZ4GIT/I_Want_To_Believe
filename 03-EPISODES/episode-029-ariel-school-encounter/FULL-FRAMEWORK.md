### THE MASS HYSTERIA EXPLANATION

**How It Could Happen:**

**Step 1: The UFO Reports (Sept 12-14):**
- **Recent sightings** across Zimbabwe (news coverage)
- **Children heard about it** (discussed at school)
- **Primed to see UFOs** (expectation set)

**Step 2: The Trigger (Sept 16):**
- **Something mundane happens:** Small aircraft, meteor, unusual light
- **One child shouts:** "UFO!"
- **Others look, see... something** (tree, bird, aircraft at distance)

**Step 3: The Feedback Loop:**
- **First child's interpretation spreads**
- **Mass suggestibility:** Children influence each other
- **Imagination fills gaps:** Brain creates details (beings, craft)
- **Panic/excitement reinforces** belief

**Step 4: Consolidation:**
- Children run to teachers
- **Retelling solidifies memory** (story becomes "real" in their minds)
- By time of interviews: **Genuinely believe** it happened

**Precedent:**
- **1983 - Devil Scare (McMartin Preschool):** Children "remembered" satanic abuse (never happenedâ€”mass suggestion)
- **1518 - Dancing Plague:** Hundreds danced uncontrollably (mass psychogenic illness)

**Skeptic Point:**
"Mass hysteria explains everything. Children susceptible, recent UFO news primed them."

### THE ADULT INFLUENCE PROBLEM

**Leading Questions:**

**Cynthia Hind (Believer):**
- **Not neutral investigator:** UFO enthusiast (wanted it to be real)
